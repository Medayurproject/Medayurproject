5 Powerful Herbs To Boost Immunity Naturally

Ancient Ayurveda secrets for stronger body defense

Why Immunity Matters?

A strong immune system helps your body fight infections, viruses and daily health problems naturally. Ayurveda has used herbs for centuries to strengthen immunity.

1. Tulsi (Holy Basil)

Rich in antioxidants. Helps fight cough, cold and infections.

2. Giloy

Known as Ayurvedic immunity king. Supports fever recovery and body strength.

3. Ashwagandha

Reduces stress and improves overall immunity performance.

4. Amla

Loaded with Vitamin C which naturally improves immune function.

5. Turmeric

Curcumin helps reduce inflammation and supports body healing.

Conclusion

Regular use of Ayurvedic herbs can naturally build strong immunity without harmful chemicals.
